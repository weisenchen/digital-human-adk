🎯 AI Meeting Host

An AI-powered meeting moderator that manages agenda, facilitates discussion, keeps time, and produces meeting minutes — purpose-built for the digital human platform.

📅 July 5, 2026 ⏱ Estimated: 5–7 days 📁 digital-human-adk
1 What Is an AI Meeting Host?

Unlike the Talk Show (host interviews guest, entertainment-focused), the Meeting Host is a productivity tool. It runs structured meetings with multiple participants, follows an agenda, keeps time, records decisions, and outputs meeting notes.

🎬 Talk Show

  • Host + 1 guest
  • Free-form interview
  • Entertainment focus
  • Sound effects, showmanship
  • No output artifact

🎯 Meeting Host NEW

  • Host + N participants
  • Structured agenda
  • Productivity focus
  • Time tracking, turn management
  • 📋 Meeting minutes output
2 Use Scenarios
Team Standup

Daily Standup

Each participant reports: what I did yesterday, what I'll do today, blockers. AI tracks who's spoken, reminds late speakers, summarizes at end.

Brainstorm

Brainstorm Session

AI presents the problem, calls on each participant for ideas, clusters themes, highlights consensus areas.

Decision Meeting

Decision / Review

AI presents agenda items, facilitates discussion, records pros/cons, states final decision, assigns action items.

3 Key Features
📋 Agenda Management Add, reorder, time-box agenda items. Each item has a title, duration, and optional notes. AI works through them in order.
👥 Participant Management List of participants with names/roles. AI calls on each person, tracks who's spoken, ensures everyone contributes.
⏱ Timer & Timekeeping Per-item countdown timer. AI warns at thresholds (2min left, 30s left). Auto-moves to next item when time's up.
✋ Turn Management Raise hand queue. AI acknowledges and calls on participants in order. Prevents crosstalk.
📝 Decision Recording AI captures key decisions, action items with assignees, and unresolved topics during the meeting.
📄 Meeting Minutes End-of-meeting summary: agenda covered, decisions made, action items, next steps. Downloadable as text/markdown.
🔴 Emergency Controls Extend item time, skip item, add agenda item on-the-fly, pause meeting.

6 AI Prompt Design

The Meeting Host prompt is a new category alongside the Talk Show prompt. Key structural differences:

Role "You are a professional meeting facilitator. Your job is to keep the meeting productive, on-track, and on-time."
Agenda List of items with durations. AI tracks progress and announces transitions.
Participants Named list. AI tracks who's spoken and proactively calls on those who haven't.
Timekeeping Per-item countdown. AI gives warnings: "2 minutes left on this item", "30 seconds — let's wrap up".
Decision Capture AI recaps: "So the decision is to proceed with Option A. Alice will draft the spec by Friday."
Output End-of-meeting structured summary: decisions, action items, next steps.
7 Implementation Plan

5 phases, estimated 5–7 days total.

📦 Phase 1: Setup UI ~1.5 days

  • Create MeetingSetup.component.tsx — meeting title, agenda items (add/remove/reorder), participant list, duration
  • Add "🎯 New Meeting" button to sidebar (between New Talk Show and New Presentation)
  • Update index.tsx to handle meeting mode state
  • Add event listener open-meeting in sidebar
  • Files: MeetingSetup/*.tsx, CyborgSidebar/*.tsx, index.tsx

⚙️ Phase 2: Backend API ~1.5 days

  • Add POST /api/meeting/ask endpoint (parallel to /api/talk-show/ask)
  • Design Meeting Host prompt with agenda, participants, timekeeping rules
  • Add POST /api/meeting/summarize — generates meeting minutes at end
  • Parse decision/action items from AI response
  • Files: server.py

🖥 Phase 3: Meeting Mode UI ~2 days

  • Create MeetingMode.component.tsx — main meeting interface
  • Left sidebar: agenda items (current highlighted, checkmarks for done)
  • Right panel: chat area (AI host + participant messages), timer display
  • Participant list with speaking status indicators
  • Raise hand button, timer warnings, progress indicator
  • End-of-meeting summary overlay with minutes download
  • Add sendMeetingMessage to adk-assistant.service.ts
  • Files: MeetingMode/*.tsx, adk-assistant.service.ts

🔊 Phase 4: Voice & Sound ~1 day

  • Support TTS for AI meeting host (reuse existing TTS pipeline)
  • Meeting-specific sound effects: gavel/open (🎯), timer warning (🔔), meeting close
  • Guest voice input (tap-to-record, reuse existing)
  • Files: MeetingMode.component.tsx, server.py (sound prompts)

📋 Phase 5: Minutes & Output ~1 day

  • Meeting minutes template: agenda, decisions, action items table
  • Download as .md or .txt
  • Option to email minutes or save to local file
  • History/recent meetings list in sidebar
  • Files: MeetingMode/*.tsx, server.py

9 Open Questions
  • Multiple participants: Should users join as live participants (type/speak), or are participants pre-defined with AI simulating their responses?
  • Meeting minutes format: Markdown, plain text, or rendered HTML?
  • Real-time clock: Should the timer be server-side or client-side?
  • Over running: If discussion is productive but time's up — auto-cut or offer extension?
  • Integration: Should meeting minutes be savable to a local notes file or sent via email?
